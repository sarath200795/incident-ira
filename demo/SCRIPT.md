## Full narration (plain text)

Welcome to Incident IRA. Incident Reporting & Analysis — a guided 5-step workflow from report to root-cause to closure.

In this short tutorial, we'll walk through how to use the app.

First, register your organization. The first person to sign up becomes the administrator.

Enter your details and a password, then create the organization.

Incident dashboard. Cross-filter by severity, type, HSE category and location; injury heatmap.

Report an incident. Step 1: date, type, severity, location, narrative and affected personnel.

Incident register. Every incident with its workflow status; resume or review at any time.

Occupational illness. Standalone illness reports — exposure, PPE, health issue and corrective actions.

Action Tracker. All corrective / preventive actions in one place, with overdue flags.

Incident IRA — report, investigate, learn.. Start by registering your organization.

That's a quick tour of Incident IRA. Thanks for watching — get started by registering your organization.
